Need suggestions for how to query the data?

Search comparative queries about tag count, unique tag count, page count, or word count.

Ex. "books with more than 500 tags", "page count > 100", "transcription shorter than 800", etc.


Search by word through tags and transcription.

Ex. "tag:bear", "transcription contains the word boy", etc.


Search by a book's metadata, such as publication year or publication location.

Ex. "published in New York", "published in 1920", etc.


Separate multiple queries using commas.

Ex. "books with more than 500 tags, fewer than 50 pages", etc.
